Increase Strength

Shoot Your Strength Through the Roof!

Follow these guidlines and you are gauranteed to increase your overall power and strength. Nothing feels better than adding some more weight to your exercises. Use these to increase athletic performance or just get stronger for the sake of being strong either way we got you covered.

Eat more Calories

Calories in vs calories out. This is the basic rule that determines whether you gain, lose, or maintain weight. if your goal is to get stronger you need more muscle, and in order to gain muscle your body has to have a surplus of calories. We recommend adding only a few hundred calories per day starting out until you gain 1lb aweek. if you increase too much you will gain excess fat along with muscle.

Lower Your Reps

Lower reps (1-6 reps) is the ideal range for powerlifting, which is centered around strength. Studies show that if you do a one rep max before your regular sets you can lift slightly heavier, increasing your exercise effectiveness. This approach also increases neuromuscular adaption,how efficient the brain is when it comes to muscle control.

Eat More Protein

Protein converts into amino acids which are the building blocks of tissue in the body, in this case that tissue is muscle. Without enough protein in your diet you lack what needed to support and maintain muscle in the body. Eating more protein also increases the rate that protein synthesis is performed. protein synthesis is the anabolic process that takes amino acis and builds muscle with them.
