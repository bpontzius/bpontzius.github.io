Summary

This routine was constructed with a focus on increasing strength. This plan requires 4 days a week and access to gym equipment. The approximate time for completion is 50 minutes. This is a beginner level but dont think that means its easy! Be sure to leave adequte time between sets as these are very taxing exercises. Space the days out resting every other day.
